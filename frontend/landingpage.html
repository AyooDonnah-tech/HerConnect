Platform features

Built for real community

HerConnect goes beyond sharing opportunities. It's a full ecosystem for sisterhood, leadership, learning, and lasting connection.

🤝

Cross-cohort Matching

Smart matching surfaces relevant sisters across all 15+ cohorts — by skill, industry, goals, and location — not just your own class.

💬

Discussion Spaces

Topic-based channels like #wins, #ask-a-sister, #tech-help, and #opportunities for real, two-way conversations — not just broadcasts.

👑

Alumni Leadership Roles

A formal leadership structure with Community Leads, Circle Captains, and Cohort Reps — alumni own and drive the ecosystem.

🌍

Sisterhood Fridays

Weekly virtual gatherings hosted by alumni. Share wins, discuss challenges, hear from role models, and celebrate each other.

📚

Resource Library

A centralized, community-curated collection of toolkits, learning paths, job templates, scholarship guides, and tech resources.

🏆

Alumni Showcase

Feature your projects, career wins, and businesses. Celebrate each other publicly. Build a legacy that inspires the next cohort.

🎓

Sister-to-Sister Mentorship

Senior cohorts mentor newer ones — a self-sustaining peer mentorship chain that grows stronger with every graduating class.

💼

Opportunities Board

Jobs, internships, scholarships, hackathons, and grants — curated for tech women, shared by the community.

🚀

Hackathon Hub

Form teams, register for challenges, and collaborate on projects. Stay connected to the builder spirit of the Code Queen Program.

Governance model

Alumni-led.
Community-owned.

HerConnect is designed for sustainability. Leadership and coordination are distributed across the community — not dependent on any single organization or team.

👑

Community Lead

Elected annually. Oversees platform direction, represents the alumni voice, and drives long-term community strategy.

1 per platform
⭐

Circle Captains

Lead thematic discussion circles — Tech, Career, Wellness, Entrepreneurship — and host weekly Sisterhood Fridays.

4–6 captains
🔶

Cohort Reps

One representative per cohort. Keeps their cohort engaged, bridges communication, and organizes cohort-specific activities.

15+ reps
📚

Resource Curators

Volunteer alumni who review and manage the resource library — keeping content relevant, fresh, and high-quality.

Volunteer role
🏛️

Self-sustaining by design. HerConnect's leadership model ensures the community continues to thrive even as individual members grow and move on. Every election cycle, new leaders emerge from within the alumni body — creating a living, self-renewing ecosystem.

For everyone

Built for your role

HerConnect serves a diverse community. Explore what the platform looks like for your role.

For Code Queen Alumni

Your program may have ended — but your community never does. HerConnect is your permanent home, no matter what cohort you graduated from.

Reconnect with your cohort and discover sisters across all 15+ classes
Find or become a mentor through the Sister-to-Sister chain
Attend Sisterhood Fridays and community events
Showcase your career wins and projects to inspire others
Take on a leadership role and help shape the community

For Current Students

You're not just in a program — you're joining a 1,000-strong sisterhood. Start building relationships and getting support from day one.

Be matched with alumni mentors from earlier cohorts
Access the resource library for learning and career prep
Join discussions and get help in #tech-help and #ask-a-sister
Attend Sisterhood Fridays before you even graduate
Join your cohort's private space and build bonds that last

For Mentors

Whether you're an ETC alumna or an external professional, HerConnect gives you a structured way to invest in the next generation of women in tech.

Create a mentor profile with your expertise and availability
Get matched with mentees based on goals and skills
Host or co-host Sisterhood Friday sessions
Contribute resources to the library
Engage in discussions and share your knowledge

For Employers & Partners

Access a verified talent pool of trained, motivated women in tech from across Africa and beyond — ready for the next step in their careers.

Post job and internship opportunities to the Opportunities Board
Browse verified alumni profiles by skill and cohort
Sponsor or participate in Sisterhood Friday events
Offer scholarships or grants through the platform
Build long-term hiring pipelines with ETC alumni

For Program Coordinators

Transition from centralized management to a self-sustaining community model — with full visibility and tools to support the ecosystem without micromanaging it.

Monitor platform engagement and community health metrics
Manage cohort onboarding and verification
Support leadership elections and governance processes
Coordinate with Circle Captains and Cohort Reps
Access analytics dashboards on alumni outcomes
